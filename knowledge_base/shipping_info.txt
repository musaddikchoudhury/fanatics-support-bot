DOMESTIC SHIPPING (Continental U.S.)
- Standard Shipping: 3-7 Business Days.
- PO Boxes/APO/FPO: 7-14 Business Days (Up to 4 weeks for APO/FPO).
- Faster Options: 2-Business Day and Next Business Day delivery available for most items.
- Carriers: UPS, LaserShip, DHL, Roadie, and UPS Mail Innovations.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING & HANDLING
- Canada: 5-10 Business Days. $12.95 shipping fee. Duties and fees (approx. 26.5%) are collected at checkout for orders over $110.
- United Kingdom: 4-10 Business Days. $29.95 shipping fee. 20% VAT charged for orders under £135; 32% Duties/VAT for orders over £135.
- Mexico: 4-10 Business Days. $29.95 shipping fee. 35% duties/fees for orders over $50.
- European Union: 4-10 Business Days. VAT and duties depend on the specific country. 12% duty rate is common.
- Other Destinations: Standard delivery is 10-20 business days; Expedited is 4-10 business days.

*Note: Transit time begins after warehouse processing time, which varies by item.
