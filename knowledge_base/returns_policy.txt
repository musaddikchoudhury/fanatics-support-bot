FANATICS RETURN POLICY OVERVIEW
Most unused merchandise can be returned up to 365 days from the item's ship date. Returns must be unworn with any original tags and labels intact.

RETURN ELIGIBILITY & CONDITIONS
- Items must be in their original condition, unworn/unused, with all tags attached.
- Clearance and Sale items listed as "FINAL SALE" or non-returnable cannot be returned.
- Customized items are final sale and cannot be returned.
- Lost, damaged, or defective items must be reported within 45 days of the shipping date.

RETURN FEES
- A $9.99 return shipping fee is deducted if you choose a refund to your original payment method using our return label or QR code.
- This fee is WAIVED if you choose a refund via an e-gift card.
- Original shipping costs are not refunded.

HOW TO START A RETURN (U.S.)
1. Go to "Track My Order" at the top of the Fanatics page.
2. Enter your Order Number and Email.
3. Select "Return Items" (if eligible).
4. Choose your return method: "Drop off at Happy Returns" (QR code, no box needed) or "Print a Label & Drop Off at UPS".
5. Refunds typically process in 2-7 business days after the item is received.

EXCHANGES
Exchanges are offered on eligible items within 60 days of the delivery date.
